Collection and use of your personal data:
Types of data collected:

Personal data:
When using our Application, you may provide information necessary for the video sharing feature to function.
Authorization data for third-party services: Telegram bot token, Telegram user ID,
Yandex email address, and application password for WebDAV access.
This data is entered manually by you and is required solely for the automatic sharing of video files.

Media Files:
The app records video with audio (background video recording).
These files are stored locally on your device in a public folder at /Android/data/com.safelogj.spines/files/video/.

Usage Data:
Usage data may include technical information about the device's state
to ensure stable background recording and file uploads.
Such as internet access (for uploading files) and whether the screen is on (to prevent the system from stopping recording when the screen is off).

Storage of Your Personal Data:
Local Storage:
Video recordings are stored on your device in a public folder at /Android/data/com.safelogj.spines/files/video/.
The authorization data you enter (Telegram tokens, WebDAV login and password) is stored in an encrypted file
in the App's internal secure directory.

Data Transfer:
The app transfers video files to third-party services (Telegram and Yandex.Disk)
only when there is an internet connection and only to the chats/storage accounts you have configured.
We (the Company) do not have access to your video files, tokens, or accounts.
All data exchange occurs directly between your device and the servers of the services you have selected (Telegram Messenger Inc. and Yandex LLC).

Deleting collected data:
Deleting video recordings: You can delete recorded files at any time using the Android file manager
from the /Android/data/com.safelogj.spines/files/video/ folder.

Deleting authorization data:
You can revoke access to Telegram or Yandex.Disk by simply deleting the data in the corresponding input fields in the App interface. When you clear data or uninstall the App, the encrypted file containing your credentials will be permanently deleted from your Device.

Deletion via Services:
You can delete files already sent to Telegram or Yandex.Disk through the respective service interfaces.
